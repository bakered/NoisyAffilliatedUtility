--- title: If Utility Is Noisy and Affiliated then there is a WTP-WTA Gap author: '' date: '' slug: [] categories: [] tags: [] weight: 3 ---

If \(i\) knows that \(j\) is willing to buy at price \(p\), then she knows that \(s_j > p\).

If \(Cov(U_i, S_j) > 1\) then \(\mathbb{E}(U_i|S_j = s_j)\) is rising in \(s_j\).

Therefore \(\mathbb{E(U_i|s_j > p) > E(U_i)}\)
